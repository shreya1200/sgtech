ABOUT US


Priyam IT is an Indian information technology company situated in Pune, India . Founded in 2006, the company employs approximately 20-25 employees with annual revenue of 2-3 crores.
The company deals in -user software for retail segment such as Grocery, Garment, Sweet Mart, Departmental Stores, Super Markets and Retailers etc for easy management of billing, inventory, bar coding and accounting e-commerce, mobile applications, cloud computing, digital transformation, data analytics, with more than 2000 active clients all over India , as of 31 May 2020.
